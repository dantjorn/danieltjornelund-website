Drawing No. 010 — Track Record

Building the capabilities organizations need for their next stage.

Across founder-led ventures, scale-ups, and board-appointed executive leadership — the same underlying work: helping organizations evolve faster than their circumstances change.

What I do

I help organizations answer the questions that matter for their next stage, then help execute against them:

  • What must we become to remain relevant?
  • Which capabilities will matter in three to five years?
  • Which parts of the business should scale, change, or disappear?
  • How should AI change the operating model?
  • How do we align people, product, governance, and commercial execution behind that future?
Core thesis
Organizations rarely fail because technology changes. They fail because organizational capability fails to evolve at the same pace.

Markets change. Customers change. Business models change. The organizations that succeed are those that continuously redesign themselves — across product, governance, and commercial execution. That is where I create value.
